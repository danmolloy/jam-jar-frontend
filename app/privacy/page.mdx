## 2. Information We Collect

We collect:
- Your email address and name when you register
- Practice data you enter, including activity logs and audio recordings
- Technical information such as browser type, device, and IP address

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and improve the Jam Jar service
- Secure your account and personal data
- Contact you about updates or issues (only if necessary)

We do **not** sell or share your data with third parties for advertising.

## 4. Audio Recordings

You may upload audio recordings. These are private to your account unless you explicitly share them. They are stored securely on Amazon S3.

## 5. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers hosted in the UK or EU. We take reasonable steps to protect your data, but no system is 100% secure.

## 6. Your Rights

If you are in the UK or EU, you have the right to:
- Access your data
- Request correction or deletion
- Withdraw consent (if applicable)

To make a request, contact us at contact@teammango.tech.

## 7. Cookies

We use cookies only where necessary (e.g., for login sessions). We do not use third-party tracking or advertising cookies.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will notify users of major changes via email or a banner on the site.
